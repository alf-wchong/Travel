# 🚨 Emergency Numbers

🇫🇮 Finland uses **112** for all emergencies.

Call **112** for:

🚑 Medical emergencies

🚓 Police

🚒 Fire

No area code is required.

---

# 🛂 I Can't Find My Passport

## 😬 First

Don't panic.

Go through this checklist:

☐ Jacket pockets

☐ Pants pockets

☐ Backpack

☐ Daypack

☐ Camera bag

☐ Coffee shop table

☐ Airport locker (if you ignored my earlier advice 😉)

Still missing?

➡️ Contact airport staff immediately.

---

# 🧳 I Can't Find My Locker

This is why we took photos.

Check:

📷 Locker number

📷 Photo of the locker wall

📷 Receipt

If you're still unsure:

ℹ️ Ask the airport information desk.

---

# 🚆 Which Train Goes Back To The Airport?

Answer:

🟢 I Train

or

🟣 P Train

Both serve Helsinki Airport.

Don't overthink it.

---

# 💳 My Credit Card Isn't Working

Try:

✅ Another card

✅ Apple Pay

✅ Google Pay

✅ Physical card instead of contactless

Still no luck?

🏧 Use an ATM.

# 📱 My Phone Battery Is Almost Dead

Priority list:

1️⃣ Charge the phone.

2️⃣ Everything else.

Your phone is:

🗺️ Navigation

🎫 Tickets

📷 Camera

💳 Wallet

✈️ Boarding pass

Without it...

Travel becomes much harder.

---

# 🌧️ The Weather Suddenly Changed

Welcome to Finland. 🇫🇮

Buy:

☔ Umbrella

or

🧥 Light jacket

Most convenience stores carry both.

---

# 🥶 I'm Colder Than I Expected

Completely normal.

Even in summer:

🌬️ Wind near the waterfront can feel much cooler than inland.

Dress in layers.
