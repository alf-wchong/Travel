# 🇫🇮 Helsinki Airport (HEL) Long Layover Survival Guide

> **Landed after a long flight?**
>
> This guide tells you exactly what to do next.

A practical, mobile-friendly handbook for **first-time visitors to Helsinki** with a **10+ hour layover**. It's designed for tired travelers who want clear, step-by-step instructions—not a traditional travel blog.

---

## ✨ Features

* ✈️ Written for first-time visitors
* 😴 Optimized for tired, sleep-deprived travelers
* 🚆 Step-by-step navigation from arrival to the city
* 🧳 Luggage locker and baggage storage guidance
* 💳 Cashless payment tips
* 🗺️ Curated walking itineraries
* 📱 Mobile-friendly Markdown
* 🎨 SVG diagrams for GitHub
* 📅 Regularly maintained

# ❤️ Design Philosophy

This repository is intentionally different from most travel guides.

Instead of providing large amounts of information, it answers one question at a time:

> **"What should I do next?"**

Every chapter is written to minimize decision fatigue after a long-haul flight.

You'll find:

* 🎯 Mission summaries
* 👀 "What you should see"
* 🚶 Step-by-step actions
* ⚠️ Common mistakes
* 💡 Practical tips
* ❤️ Travel wisdom
* 📋 Checklists
* 🎨 Visual diagrams
